Wild Ark

Check out the photos and learn about the species from around the world.

The idea for Wild Ark was seeded seeded in in the life of Upamanyu Das, in his boyhood. Inspired by the work of Joel Sartore, the Wild Ark is a personal project similar to Photo Ark to document every species living in zoos and wildlife sanctuaries (around 15,000 species). Think of this as a 2nd Ark that is being build. Explore thephotos in the Ark to find out more about wildlife around the world and the threats they face.
